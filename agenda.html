City Agenda

City Agenda is a mobile service that updates the citizen with the schedule of events occurring around him.

City Agenda is a mobile service that aims to increase the citizen participation by keeping them updated with the schedule of the events. The objective is to develop a mobile solution that allows the users to look for cultural and leisure events –by theme– happening in Barcelona. The application provides a list of events occurring within an established distance, geo-location of the user, map of events, search tool for events and possibility of highlighting events.
